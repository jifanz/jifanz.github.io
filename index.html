I am a Ph.D. student in computer science at the University of Wisconsin. My research focuses on both theoretical and applied perspectives of Machine Learning. I am working on active learning and deep learning problems with Robert D. Nowak.

I obtained my M.S. and B.S. degrees in computer science from the University of Washington. During my time, I have been fortunate to be advised by Kevin Jamieson, Lalit Jain, Tanner Schmidt, Dieter Fox and Zachary Tatlock.
